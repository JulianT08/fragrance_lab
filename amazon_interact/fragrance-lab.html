Understanding the Scent Pyramid

Every fragrance unfolds in three acts — each note layer evaporates at a different rate, revealing the full composition over time.

Top Notes0–15 min
Heart Notes30 min–4 hrs
Base Notes4+ hrs

Top notes create the first impression but fade fastest. The heart defines character. Base notes — sandalwood, vanilla, amber — anchor the composition and are what people remember.
